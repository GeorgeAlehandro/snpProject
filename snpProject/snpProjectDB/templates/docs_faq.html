Frequently Asked Questions

What is the goal of this website?

The goal of this page is to look up for SNPs and traits in an interactive way. The serverside calculations and filtering help in making the data fetch light and easy for the users. The power of datatables!

What is the source of the data?

The data available is initially based on roughly 35000 associations that are then mapped into an internal database containing many tables. These associations are based on GWAS studies mentioned in the scientif articles.

Can I help in building the database?

Please do! Insert your data by logging in and then heading to the bring your data page. Your submission will then be curated by a database maintainer.
